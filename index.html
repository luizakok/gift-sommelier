Gift Sommelier

An AI agent that remembers your friends' birthdays, recommends curated gifts, and delivers the perfect message — all through Telegram.

The Problem & Solution

The need it solves

Remembering birthdays, choosing thoughtful gifts, and following through on sending them requires time, memory, and decision-making energy most people don't have. The result: late gifts, generic choices, or nothing at all — despite genuine care for the person.

What the agent does

Gift Sommelier monitors upcoming birthdays from a personal contact list, proactively reaches out via Telegram, presents curated gift options based on the recipient's personality, and generates a personalized card message — turning a multi-step task into a single conversation.
